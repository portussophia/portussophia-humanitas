    <h2 id="humanitas-title">It began with a small act of truthfulness.</h2>
    <p>Baltimore, Maryland. October 23, 2025.</p>
    <p>A man walked from MCVET to the Enoch Pratt Free Library on Orleans Street. The sky was perfectly blue. The air was exactly right. He noted the time: 11:11.</p>
    <p>He missed the inscription window by seven minutes and recorded it anyway.</p>
    <p>Not with axioms. Not with a framework. Not with research. With a man who told the truth about a small thing when no one would have known the difference.</p>

*A visible record of what was later bounded, corrected, superseded, or declined without rewriting the earlier artifact.*

No public correction records have been admitted to this repository yet.

Corrections belong beside the historical artifact rather than being silently substituted into it. The sealed object remains unchanged; a correction record explains the later boundary, disposition, or supersession relation.

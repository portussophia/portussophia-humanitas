PortusHumanitas publishes the provenance records already attached to its sealed public artifacts. It does not create a second release authority.

## What witness establishes

A ledger entry, witness statement, hash, or seal may establish that:

- a particular artifact existed in a particular form;
- its bytes match a declared digest;
- a named process or witness recorded its standing;
- a later public copy remains identical to the sealed object.

These records do not independently establish truth, endorsement, peer review, or correctness.
